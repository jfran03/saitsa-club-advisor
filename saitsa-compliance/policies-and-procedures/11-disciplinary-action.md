**11.1.** Disciplinary action shall be taken against a club if:
- **11.1.1** The club violates Saitsa and/or SAIT's governing documents;
- **11.1.2** The club abuses SAIT or Saitsa services, privileges or funding;
- **11.1.3** The club engages in inappropriate conduct.

**11.2.** An individual wishing to report a perceived club violation may contact the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs who then shall notify the Manager, Student Experience.

**11.3.** Any disciplinary concerns brought to the Manager, Student Experience shall be reviewed with the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs. If immediate action is required, the Manager, Student Experience may immediately suspend club privileges until the issue is resolved.

**11.4.** The club shall be notified of the decision of the Manager, Student Experience within five (5) business days following their decision.

**11.5.** Disciplinary actions may include, but are not limited to:
- Suspension of all club funds
- Inability to apply for future Saitsa funding
- Termination of individuals' member status
- Restriction of club benefits
- Registration revocation

**11.6.** Should a club have their registration revoked, the club shall:
- **11.6.1** Surrender all assets (finances, equipment, locker, etc.) to Saitsa;
- **11.6.2** Forfeit its ability to apply for club registration for the remainder of the fiscal year.

---

### Appeal Process

**11.7.** A club may appeal the decision of the Manager, Student Experience regarding any disciplinary action. The process is as follows:
- **11.7.1** A letter must be submitted to the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) via email from a Club Executive within **two (2) weeks** of being notified of the Manager's decision;
- **11.7.2** The CEO shall review the appeal. Should the CEO deem the appeal to have grounds, they should meet with the club executives and Manager, Student Experience separately to gather information and come to a decision;
- **11.7.3** The CEO shall notify the club executives of their decision within **two (2) weeks** of their meeting with the respective parties involved.

**11.8.** The CEO reserves the right to uphold or overturn any decision of the Manager, Student Experience.
