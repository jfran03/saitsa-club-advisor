**10.1.** A club may include the following individuals:
- **10.1.1** **Voting Member:** An individual who is an active student who has paid their Saitsa fees within the current academic year.
- **10.1.2** **Non-voting Member:** An individual who has not paid their Saitsa fees within the current academic year.
- **10.1.3** **Member Staff:** An individual who is either an employee, agent, contractor, or volunteer of Saitsa who are simultaneously voting members.

**10.2.** Clubs can endorse or support candidates of their choice, excepting that directors and member staff shall remain neutral during their official engagements or working hours.

**10.3.** Clubs shall not allow non-voting members to either campaign in or influence the outcome of the annual general election, by-elections or membership referenda.

**10.4.** To host any election outreach events and/or opportunities, Clubs must comply with all requirements within the Elections & Referenda Policies & Procedures document and the following:
- **10.4.1** Any election outreach and/or opportunities must only occur during the elections campaign period and only allow candidates to promote their campaign to voting members.
- **10.4.2** Any election outreach and/or opportunities hosted by a Club must provide equal opportunity for each candidate to participate.
- **10.4.3** All election outreach and/or opportunities must be pre-approved by the Chief Returning Officer (CRO) in writing a minimum of **48 hours** prior to such events and/or opportunities. Clubs must provide the CRO in writing with a minimum of the following details to receive approval:
  - a. Date
  - b. Time
  - c. Location
  - d. Agenda of the event and/or opportunity
