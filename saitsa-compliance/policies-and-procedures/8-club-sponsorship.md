### Eligibility

**8.1.** Student Clubs seeking sponsorship must be officially recognized and registered with Saitsa through the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs.

**8.2.** Student Clubs must demonstrate a clear mission, goals, and activities that align with the educational objectives and values of Saitsa and SAIT.

---

### Application

**8.3.** Student Clubs are to prepare different levels of sponsorship packages based on the club's size, activities, and demonstrated impact on the campus community and submit to the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs prior to seeking sponsorship.

**8.4.** The sponsorship package should include:
- **8.4.1** Financial support your club requires.
- **8.4.2** Services your club will provide for the sponsor such as: publicity and promotion, networking, connections, among others.

**8.5.** Clubs seeking sponsorship must submit an application to the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs at the beginning of each academic year.

**8.6.** The Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs will review all applications and evaluate them based on the eligibility criteria and available resources to Saitsa and SAIT institutional policies and guidelines.

**8.7.** The decision regarding sponsorship will be communicated to the club leadership within a reasonable timeframe.

**8.8.** If a club's sponsorship application is approved, a sponsorship agreement will be drafted and signed by the club leadership and the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs.

**8.9.** The agreement will outline the terms and conditions of the sponsorship, including the financial support amount, the club's responsibilities, reporting requirements, and adherence to Saitsa and SAIT institutional policies and guidelines.

**8.10.** Any changes or modifications to the sponsorship agreement must be mutually agreed upon by both parties and documented in writing.

**8.11.** Sponsored clubs are required to provide periodic progress reports to the Student Experience Coordinator, Clubs, detailing their activities and the impact they have achieved.

**8.12.** Saitsa may conduct periodic evaluations to assess the effectiveness of the sponsorship and ensure that the club is meeting its stated goals and adhering to institutional policies.

**8.13.** Sponsorships are typically granted for one academic year, and clubs must reapply for sponsorship at the beginning of each academic year.

**8.14.** Renewal of sponsorship will be based on the club's demonstrated performance, impact, and adherence to institutional policies.
