**3.1.** Club membership shall be open and inclusive to all SAIT students; Clubs may be subject to disciplinary action for discriminating against community members wishing to join their club.

**3.2.** Members of the community, including SAIT and Saitsa staff and faculty, are eligible to become members at the discretion of the club's executives; however, they cannot act as club executives or hold voting rights.

**3.3.** Club membership must consist of a minimum of seventy-five per cent (75%) of current SAIT students.

**3.4.** Clubs are permitted, but not required, to charge membership fees. Club membership fees are to be determined by club executives and deposited into the club's bank account.

**3.5.** To become a member of a club, individuals shall:

- **3.5.1** Contact the Club President or designate and request to become a member;
- **3.5.2** Fulfill all obligations as prescribed by the club's membership requirements (attend meetings, pay membership fee, etc.).
