## Saitsa Clubs Portal

Welcome to Saitsa Clubs! Join an existing club or start your own! The Saitsa Rubric Clubs Portal is a resource for Saitsa Student Clubs, offering everything you need for club registration, events, activities, merchandise and opportunities.

Students can discover all the opportunities and events provided by SAIT departments, Saitsa departments and Saitsa Clubs, on the Saitsa website and Rubric Campus app.

Students will need to sign-in using their SAIT credentials to gain access.

> **Portal:** [Portal.HelloRubric.com](https://Portal.HelloRubric.com)

---

### How to Access the Portal

1. Visit [Portal.HelloRubric.com](https://Portal.HelloRubric.com)
2. New clubs can register right away and select SAIT from the university dropdown. For existing clubs, please contact the SECC at saitsa.clubs@edu.sait.ca for further instructions.

---

### How to Start a New Club

- Any currently enrolled student at SAIT is eligible to establish or join a student club.
- Clubs may register at any time during the fiscal year; however, clubs are permitted to change their club name **at registration only**.
- Before you register, make sure you have at least **three (3) current key club executive positions filled**: the President, a Vice-President Operations, and a Vice-President Finance. Please note that only current SAIT students who have paid their Saitsa fee may hold executive positions.

**Registration Steps:**

1. Fill out the **Stage 1 Registration form**. It will first prompt you to create a new Rubric club account which you can proceed with using your SAIT email address.
2. After completing the Stage 1 form, you can proceed to complete and submit the **Stage 2 and 3 forms**, which will both be accessible from your new Rubric Club account.

> Please note, Saitsa reserves the right to refuse any club application that violates Saitsa's governing documents.

---

### How to Re-register a Club

Re-registration is an important process to ensure your club remains recognized and supported by Saitsa.

**Steps to re-register your club each year:**

1. Log in to your Rubric account at [Campus.HelloRubric.com](https://Campus.HelloRubric.com)
2. Click on your profile, switch to the club account using your executive access.
3. Navigate to the **Forms module** and select the **Re-registration Form**.
4. Complete the form with your club's updated information.
5. The designated club bank account administrators need to submit the following documents **separately**:
   - A government-issued photo ID (e.g., Driver's License or Passport)
   - SAIT ID
   - Proof of enrolment: Any screenshot from the Student Profile section under the MyStudent tab in your MySAIT account, confirming your Active status.
6. **Oath of Responsibility** for all Saitsa Club Executives: Kindly ensure all club executives complete and sign this form.

> Kindly contact the SECC at Saitsa.clubs@edu.sait.ca if you don't have executive access to the club account.

### How to Join a Club

1. Visit [Campus.HelloRubric.com](https://Campus.HelloRubric.com)
2. Select the club and membership type to join the club.
3. Sign-in using your SAIT credentials to complete the process and join the club.

> The Saitsa Clubs team is available to help you establish your club. For questions, contact: **Saitsa.clubs@edu.sait.ca**
